# Product Guidelines

## Prose & Narrative Style
- **Informative & Clear**: Content should be structured to deliver news accurately and clearly, ensuring that the listener receives the essential facts without ambiguity.
- **Minimalist**: Avoid unnecessary fluff or filler; the narrative should get straight to the core content of the article.

## Audio Branding & Voice
- **Neutral & Objective**: Maintain a standard news reporting style that is objective and unbiased, suitable for professional journalism.
- **Warm & Engaging**: Balance objectivity with voices that are approachable and human-like to keep the listener engaged throughout the audio.

## User Experience Principles
- **Simplicity & Speed**: Prioritize a "single-click" experience where the user can go from URL to audio with minimal manual effort and maximum speed.
- **Transparency**: Be transparent about the system's decisions, providing insights into how text was extracted and how specific pronunciations were determined.

## Operational Principles
- **Accuracy First**: The system must prioritize the accuracy of text extraction and the correctness of phonetic pronunciations above all else.
- **Feedback-Driven**: Implement and respect feedback loops to constantly refine the AI models for extraction, structuring, and pronunciation based on real-world usage.
